About

Mabuhay! We are BINI !

A Filipino female group called Bini (stylized as BINI) formed in 2019 through ABS-CBN's Star Hunt Academy. The group's name was taken from the Filipino word binibini which means "young lady", a nod to the group's vision to "embody the idea of a modern Filipina — sweet, fierce, independent, and informed." Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena make up the group of eight. Bini made their formal debut with the song "Born to Win" on June 11, 2021, following two years of training at Star Hunt Academy. With the hits "Salamin, Salamin" and "Pantropiko," the girls have swiftly risen to the top of the Filipino music charts, making them the largest female artist in history and the country's second-biggest artist overall.

Recently, their listeners have immensely grew and has now reached 7 million streams monthly on the Spotify platform alone.
